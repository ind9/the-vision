Product Card

To showcase product tiles

Usage

The product card is a consistent reperesentation of a product with it's most critical attributes. The display is quite flexible to adapt to most common use cases.

Code: 

var productCard = <ProductCard
          title="Asus Zenfone"
          price="$300"
          image="http://img6a.flixcart.com/imae9tftbfdcxzng.jpeg"
          link="http://www.flipkart.com/asus-zenfone"/>;
          

          ReactDOM.render(productCard, document.getElementById('#product-card-example'));
